Biography

I am a senior Undergraduate at IIT Kanpur. I am broadly interested in Computer Vision , it’s intersection with Image Processing and in general Intelligence. Currently, I’m working at Prof. Yoichi Sato Lab at University of Tokyo, Tokyo on Future localization in First Person Vision. Also, I have worked with Prof. Tanaya Guha at Signal and Multi-media Processing Lab, IIT Kanpur on Dyadic Srynchrony Detection using Audi-visual Clues. This project is still in progress.
Previous summers, I had the opputunity to work with Prof. K S Venkatesh on Image procsssing using Celluar Automaton in 2016 Summers under the Students-Undergraduate Research and Graduate Excellence (SURGE) program. The results were published in the IEEE conference - ‘Irish Signals and Systems Conference’ held in June 2017.
Apart from my academic endeavours, I am a budding philosopher and absolutely love an interesting book/chat/discussion on different viwpoints of rationality, ethics and self-conduct. I am also an active memeber of the Counselling Service at IIT Kanpur of which I was a Core Team Member (2016 - 2017) and like to spend my leisure giving back to the community through community work. Not too much of a sport enthusiast, I sparingly play Table Tennis and Go.
